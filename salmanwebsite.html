About GULMIT

Gulmit is a centuries-old village with gigantic mountains, peaks, and glaciers. The most beautiful ever village situated in the Gojal valley in the upper Hunza region of northern Pakistan. Known as the "valley of flowers," Gulmit is a true paradise full of natural beauty and rich cultural heritage.

Attractions

Gulmit offers a variety of attractions for visitors, including:

  • Gulmit Tower Peak
  • Shutabur Meadows
  • Kamaris Rock Climbing
  • Traditional Polo Ground
  • Historic Mosques and Libraries
